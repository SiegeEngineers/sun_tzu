**Deer Pushing Procedure:**

1. **Villager Preparation:**
   - Have at least two villagers positioned under the town center.

2. **Scout and Control Group:**
   - Select the scout and create a control group for efficient management.

3. **Alignment of Points:**
   - Ensure alignment of three points: Town center, deer, and pushing position behind the deer.

4. **Execution:**
   - With the scout selected, press 'E' and click on the deer.
   - Alternatively, move the scout behind the deer without the follow command.

5. **Villager Assistance:**
   - If receiving villagers are out of range or risk deterring the deer, garrison them and un-garrison to quickly rearrange the deer.

**Note:** Maintain these steps for effective deer pushing in your gameplay.
