To ease into multiplayer, it's essential to have a basic Dark Age build order, a main civilization or two, and knowledge of basic unit counters. For online etiquette, communication, and understanding common conventions like pocket and flank players in team games are crucial. Learning pausing etiquette is also important.

For a smooth transition, start by playing against the AI, then move to unranked lobbies, and eventually, try ranked games. Quick play and ranked options are available, with ratings helping match you with similarly skilled opponents. One versus one ratings average around 1,000, while team game ratings average around 1,450.

Overall, the online community is diverse, and it's important to approach online play with a mindset of having fun. The community tends to be mature and supportive. Give online a try and enjoy the experience!
